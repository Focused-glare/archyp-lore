---

Dear readers! If you still naively believe that "management" is when a gray-haired collective farm chairman screams into a telephone receiver at obedient brigadiers, then you are hopelessly stuck in the last century. Progress does not stand still! 

In our bright year of **2076**, the management process has finally reached its absolute, crystalline peak. We have "managed" (or, as they say in the IT-container world, "dockered") ourselves to death, achieving complete and uncompromising harmony.

Welcome to our report from the streets of a typical mega-hub of the New Era!

---

### The "Tumbleweed" Citizen

How was it before? This backward, petty-bourgeois psychology of *"my home is my castle"*, *"a cherry orchard near the cottage"*, graves of ancestors, roots... Ugh, it makes one's heart ache to think of such economic inefficiency! 

The modern conscious layman of the 2076 model is a proud, light, and extremely mobile **Tumbleweed**. 

*   **Nothing of one's own:** Why should a human carry an extra burden in the form of their own housing or, God forbid, a plot of land? All property has been successfully optimized and converted into a subscription format. 
*   **Life for rent:** Everything is rented—from a sleeping capsule measuring two by one meter to a synthetic cloak. 
*   **Biomass routing:** If the algorithm in the cloud decides that your efficiency coefficient in this sector has dropped by 1.5%, your capsule subscription is simply canceled. You are politely offered a free one-way ticket to the nearest industrial enclave. The person takes their virtual QR code and rolls along with the wind. No tears, no nostalgia, no roots. Crystalline beauty!

---

### Digital Caring Comfort

A special pride of our era is the concern for safety. The procedure of buying a regular ticket for a suburban vacuum train has turned into an exciting intellectual quest.

> "Would you like to travel two stops? Sure! Only first enter into the terminal your great-grandmother's maiden name, your second cousin's blood type, the identification number of your reproductive paper, and undergo a five-minute retinal scan. No, no, we do not accept cash—it's a dirty source of financial anarchy! Only clean digital, only full transparency under the supervision of our beloved cloud."

Of course, some unconscious citizens try to poke fake data into the screen. But the system smiles sweetly with the lenses of surveillance cameras: we still know who you are, dear user, by your digital footprint!

---

### Industrial Paradise without Extra Noise

And what is happening in those vast territories where golden fields once waved and human villages bustled? Now there is silence, peace, and grace. No noisy local population that is always demanding something—roads, hospitals, clean rivers. 

*   **Staff optimization through complete mechanization!** Huge quarries and endless automated fields are serviced exclusively by iron machines under the guidance of cloud dispatchers. 
*   **Rotational sterility:** Once every six months, a hermetic shuttle arrives with a handful of highly specialized engineers. They live in closed modules, never going outside without spacesuits, executing their technological instructions, and quietly departing back. 
*   **Reliable protection:** If some random traveler without a QR passport tries to approach the area of robotic subsoil extraction, they will be met by a polite but very determined swarm of autonomous drones. The land works for the benefit of the transnational budget without any demographic obstacles.

---

### Love according to Government Standard

And of course, we cannot fail to mention the radical improvement of personal life. Those ancient foolishnesses with serenades under windows, flowers, jealousy, and long courtships are finally declared harmful to public health. 

Now everything works according to a strict reproductive contract:

1.  A healthy male gene pool without genetic markers of anxiety and free-thinking is declared a strategic resource. 
2.  A woman who wants to get an heir no longer hopes for blind chance or romantic nonsense. She submits an official application through the "Gov-Reproduction" app. 
3.  She receives certified, loyalty-tested biomaterial from a specially selected donor, who is kept in a closed boarding house under close guard. 

Fast, hygienic, and most importantly—no unpredictable emotional outbursts and family dramas!

---

### Instead of an Epilogue

The world of 2076 has become incredibly convenient. There is no room for surprises, rebellions, or complex philosophical reflections. Society no longer needs to understand how this "global theater" works. The main thing is that the feed of personalized content in your virtual glasses is updated on time, and there is always the right dose of soothing neuroregulators in your drinking water.

So relax, dear users. You have already been thought of, you have already been calculated, and you have been completely... **managed**!
